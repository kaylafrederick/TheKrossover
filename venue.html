Parking

At The Krossover, we want your concert experience to be as smooth as possible, starting with parking. Our venue offers ample parking space for guests, with both on-site and nearby parking options available.
On-Site Parking:
We offer a convenient parking lot directly adjacent to the venue, providing easy access to the main entrance. The lot is open to all concertgoers and is free of charge. It fills up quickly, so we recommend arriving early for the best spots!
Additional Parking:
If the on-site lot is full, there are several nearby parking garages and street parking options within walking distance of The Krossover. Look for signs directing you to additional parking located just a short distance from the venue.
Accessible Parking:
For guests with disabilities, we have designated accessible parking spots located near the venue's main entrance to ensure a hassle-free experience.
Please plan accordingly and arrive early to secure your parking spot. We look forward to seeing you at the show!

Bags & Carry-ins

To ensure the safety and enjoyment of all our guests, The Krossover has specific guidelines regarding bags and carry-ins. Please review the following rules before attending an event:
Bag Policy:
For the safety and convenience of all concertgoers, large bags, backpacks, and oversized items are not permitted inside the venue. Small bags (up to 12” x 12”) are allowed but must be subject to inspection upon entry. Please note that all bags will be checked at security before entering the venue.
Prohibited Items:
The following items are strictly prohibited and cannot be carried into the venue:

  • Large backpacks, suitcases, or any items that obstruct security checks
  • Outside food or beverages (except for those with medical necessity)
  • Alcohol or illegal substances
  • Weapons, fireworks, or other hazardous materials

Carry-Ins:
Personal items such as wallets, phones, and small cameras are permitted, but please refrain from bringing any items that may disturb the show or other attendees. We recommend leaving unnecessary items at home for a more enjoyable and streamlined experience.
We appreciate your cooperation in helping us maintain a safe and welcoming environment for all. Enjoy the show!
