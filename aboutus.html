Welcome to The Krossover, where every show is an unforgettable experience! We are a premier concert venue located in the heart of Folly Beach, South Carolina, dedicated to bringing the best live music and entertainment to our community. Whether you're here to see world-renowned artists or discover up-and-coming talent, The Krossover is the place where music and magic collide.
Founded in 2023, our mission is simple: to provide a unique and immersive live music experience for fans of all genres. From indie to rock, electronic to pop, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse lineup that appeals to music lovers from all walks of life.
Our state-of-the-art venue features stunning acoustics, a spacious stage, and an intimate atmosphere that allows our audience to feel connected to the music in a way that only live performances can provide. With top-notch sound systems, lighting, and a welcoming environment, every concert at The Krossover is an experience you'll never forget.
Come for the music, stay for the memories.
